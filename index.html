About us

Welcome to the Cinnamon Hotel, a magnificent haven where comfort and opulence blend to provide a unique hospitality experience. Our upscale boutique hotel caters to discerning tourists seeking both peace and adventure. It is a symbol of sophisticated living. Our business encourages you to set off on a voyage of enjoyment and exploration as it is tucked away among the colorful landscapes of Sri Lanka and the Maldives.You'll be surrounded by a sophisticated and cozy atmosphere the moment you enter our facilities. Each painstakingly created room and suite perfectly combines modern elegance with the unique attraction of the local way of life, guaranteeing a stay that is both aesthetically entrancing and emotionally reviving.

Enjoy a remarkable gastronomic journey at one of our top-notch restaurants, where every mouthful celebrates the region's many tastes. Whether you are traveling for business or pleasure, our committed team is fully committed to planning a flawless stay for you.Immerse yourself in the fascination of two extraordinary locations as you enjoy Cinnamon Hotel's seamless fusion of contemporary opulence and old-world hospitality. We guarantee that your stay with us will be a memorable chapter in your trip memoir.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation at your hotel?

You can easily make a reservation by visiting our website and using our online booking system. Alternatively, you can contact our reservations team via phone or email for assistance.

Do you offer special rates for extended stays?

Yes, we do offer special rates for guests planning extended stays. Please reach out to our reservations team to inquire about our long-term stay packages.

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Our standard check-in time is at 3:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM. If you need early check-in or late check-out, please contact our front desk, and we will do our best to accommodate your request based on availability.

Is Wi-Fi available at the hotel, and is it complimentary?

Yes, we offer complimentary Wi-Fi access throughout the hotel, including guest rooms and common areas.

Do you have on-site parking, and is there a fee?

We provide on-site parking for our guests. Please check our "Parking" page for details on parking rates, if applicable.
