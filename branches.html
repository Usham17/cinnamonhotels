Cinnamon Bentota Beach

Cinnamon Bentota Beach is the perfect tropical paradise location on the southern coast of Sri Lanka, nestled along the captivating Indian Ocean and overlooking gorgeous expanses of palm-fringed beaches and a flowing river. This opulent beachfront resort's history dates back to the 1960s, when Geoffrey Manning Bawa, one of Asia's most well-known architects, brilliantly created its original vision.

The resort, which is on the upscale stretch known as the "Gold Coast," has the greatest water sports facility in the region and is adjacent to a number of tourist destinations, historical monuments, and cultural icons.

Cinnamon Wild Yala

We are overjoyed to see our wild neighbors and can feel your delight. Yes, due to our environment of the jungle, we have evolved the sixth sense. We hope to see you soon, but it's possible that some of our wild companions will welcome you first because the road you must travel to get to us is also the trail they go for a nighttime stroll. In reality, we arrange for romantic meals to be served on a terrace by the lake under a starry sky, and we promise you that it is lovely. Cinnamon Citadel Kandy

Welcome to Cinnamon Citadel Kandy, a haven of peace surrounded by the Mahaweli River and the Knuckles Mountain Range.

The resort's vantage position, which is surrounded by thick vegetation and colorful blossoms, offers visitors panoramic views that highlight the unadulterated beauty of nature. The adjacent mountain and river offer nature lovers a choice of distinctive adventures, from yoga and walks to boat trips and daring water-based sports. The respected Temple of the Tooth, which attracts tourists interested in the fascinating local history, is also the location's principal attraction.

Our resort is a doorway to Sri Lanka's colorful traditions and arts, offering an all-encompassing cultural experience around you. Do so right now.

Trinco Blu by Cinnamon

Welcome to Trinco Blu by Cinnamon, a tranquil beach retreat tucked away in Trincomalee, Sri Lanka.

The resort provides a variety of guided nature paths that lead to thrilling whale-watching opportunities and the chance to discover undersea treasures through snorkeling adventures for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Trinco Blu by Cinnamon, created for travelers looking for adventure and sunlight, provides a seaside getaway and a break from the fast-paced metropolitan lifestyle. Do so right now.
