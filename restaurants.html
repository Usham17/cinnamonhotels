Café C - Cinnamon Citadel, Kandy.

The tranquil, cozy, and romantic ambiance of Café C, which is located close to the river, is the ideal setting for a romantic date night. Additionally, with a maximum capacity of 60 guests, it is a perfect location for private parties and small business get-togethers.
