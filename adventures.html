Adventures Unveiled

Discover unforgettable journeys with us. From thrilling escapades to cultural immersions, our adventures page is your gateway to extraordinary experiences

Adventure Awaits - Join Life With Us

Welcome to the heart of the adventure! Here at Cinnamon, we're all about the thrill of the unknown, the fun of pushing limits, and the excitement of adventure.

Our goal is to ignite the spark of adventure in everyone, encouraging them to embark on extraordinary journeys. We offer a range of experiences, from adrenaline-pumping activities to tranquil escapes into nature.

Our team of seasoned adventurers and outdoor enthusiasts is dedicated to curating the most exhilarating and awe-inspiring experiences.

We believe that life's greatest stories are written during moments of adventure, and we're here to help you script your own epic tale. Join us on a journey of a lifetime together, starting at Cinnamon

Choose Your Adventure

Colombo National Museum

Unveil the treasures of ancient Sri Lankan history.

This historical museum transports you back in time and is housed inside a grand colonial palace surrounded by lovely gardens. Explore the many exhibitions that highlight the amazing architecture, fascinating history, and fine art of this magnificent island nation at your own pace.

Old Parliament

Capture the essence of colonial history.

Take a stroll inside this Neo-Baroque marvel of architecture, which provides insight into Sri Lanka's colonial past. This gorgeous location emanates warmth and old-world elegance and is surrounded by a tranquil garden decorated with bronze statues of notable figures.

Vihara Mahadevi Park

Watch the world go by.

Enter a peaceful, sizable park, and you'll find peace among the city's activity. Admire the stunning figure of Lord Buddha as it is around by impeccably kept plants, man-made ponds, and fountains that create a serene atmosphere.

Water Skiing

Glide through the endless.

The river serves as inspiration for the unending elegance that permeates every area of our hotel. Why not encounter it at the source? Water skiing over the endless sea will allow you to understand why we hold ourselves to such high standards.

Disco Donut Rides

Whirl through the ocean.

You may have already realized that we have an ocean full of entertainment for you, so you'll never be stuck for things to do in Bentota. From thrill coasters to joy rides, there are rides for every level of excitement. The latter category might include the disco donut rides.

Ambalangoda Mask Factory & Museum

Unmask the island's beautiful art and culture.

The traditional arts and cultural sector of Sri Lanka is incredibly distinctive. The Ambalangoda Mask Museum has long served as its true representative. When you enter this sanctuary of old culture, interesting masks will be looking back at you.

Sunrise at Adam's Peak

Experience the beauty of sunrise at its peak.

Lord Buddha's, Adam's, and Lord Shiva's footprints are said to be on the rock at this holy location, also known as Sri Pada.

Whale Watching in Mirissa

An extraordinary marine adventure awaits you.

Enjoy the amazing chance to see playful dolphins and whales in their natural environment. It's like seeing a flawlessly staged play in the water as these majestic animals gracefully dive into and out of the ocean.

Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic

A world-renowned landmark in Kandy.

The Relic of the Buddha's Tooth is kept safe in the respected location known as the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic on Sri Dalada Veediya in Kandy.

Dambulla Cave Temple

Immerse yourself in Sri Lankan culture.

Visit the well-known Dambulla cave temple, a holy pilgrimage destination that is Sri Lanka's largest and best-preserved cave-temple complex.

Bird and Butterfly Trail

Of bird songs and kaleidoscopes.

We are surrounded by a well planned ecology at Habarana Lodge by Cinnamon, which welcomes nature in all its manifestations. Thankfully, nature reacted in a cheery, vibrant way. Seasonally, 48 different bird species and 50 distinct types of butterflies converge on our home. So, get up early and take a tour around our vast, beautiful gardens to find them all.

Yala National Park Safari

Experience the thrill of the wild.

Welcome to Yala National Park, a mostly desert region with the world's greatest concentration of leopards! While the elusive cat is the main attraction, this vast area of wonder is also home to over 44 different kinds of animals and 215 different bird species.

Polonnaruwa Tour

Feel time coming to a standstill.

Travel back in time to a time of exquisite art and architecture by taking a bicycle tour of Polonnaruwa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Start by visiting the Archaeological Museum, which is home to a wonderful collection of bronze statues, some antiquated medical equipment, and a topographical model of the city as it would have been in the past.

Marine Mammal Safari

Witness the rhythmic dance of dolphins and whales.

Watch dolphins and whales gracefully dance in turquoise seas on a heart-stopping marine mammal safari. The prime months for whale and dolphin viewing are March to April, while the prime months for dolphin watching are May to October.

Snorkeling

An intimate encounter with life beneath waters.

Journey into the captivating realm of the sea in the Vaavu Atoll.

Malé Submarine

An underwater voyage and city adventure

Experience the Malé Submarine for a one-of-a-kind naval journey. If you dive 50 meters below, you may see the lovely aquatic creatures drifting by.

Residential Island Tour to Muli & Nalafushi

Understand local culture from a fresh perspective.

Engage in deep conversations with the people while exploring the vibrant islands of Naalaafushi, another adjacent inhabited island, and Muli, the capital of the Meemu Atoll.

Dolphin safari

Discover playful Dolphin pods.

Observe Spinner dolphins doing an elegant dance as they play in the North Ari Atoll's crystal-clear waters.
